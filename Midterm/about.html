About me.

CS and I.
I am a Computer Science student at Gonzaga University expected to graduate in May 2019. I have studied abroad in Auckland, New Zealand to further study computer science. CS has been a major part of my life since senior year of high school when I first was introduced to Java programming in an AP CS class. Since then I have dabbled in hardware programming and design, mobile app development, machine learning, and database services. I am extremely eager to learn any facet of computer science development and have experimented in multiple fields because of it.

Besides computer science I am interested in biology and mathematics, enough so to get a minor in math and to fill some of my core science with biology. Health and human physiology are both major interests of mine and so I eventually hope to work as a CS developer in those fields.
